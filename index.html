Why people drive to Mack Moore Shoes

This is not a big box shoe store. Mack Moore is a sit‑and‑fit store – you get measured, fitted, and matched with brands that actually support how you move and work.

  • Family-owned, local Charleston business

Fit & comfort focused

We specialize in hard‑to‑fit feet.

If you struggle with off‑the‑shelf shoes, we work with brands and styles that give more depth, support, and stability. Many customers with diabetes or prosthetics rely on Mack Moore to keep them walking comfortably.

For workplaces and larger crews, Mack Moore can also bring a mobile truck to your company site on a scheduled date so employees can be fitted and buy work shoes on‑site by appointment.
